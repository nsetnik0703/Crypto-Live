Discovering Associations In The CryptoCurrency Ecosytem


This is a web application that explores possible short-term correlations between the top six market-valued cryptocurrencies according to their exchange rates. Here, exchange rates relative to bitcoin are displayed from the past 7 days as well as their most recent recorded exchange rate. In the 'Explore' page, the correlations can be observed between two cryptocurrencies by a specific time period with descriptive statistics and Pearson correlations. In the 'History' page, historical trends of USD valuations and descriptive summaries of each cryptocurrency are displayed, based on user choice. In the 'Comparison' page, we provide the USD valuation of bitcoin itself.
